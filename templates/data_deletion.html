Data Deletion Instructions

If you are an Instagram user who has messaged @himanistanbul and wish to have your message data deleted from our systems, please send an email to privacy@hi-man.com.tr with the subject “Instagram Data Deletion Request”. Include your Instagram handle and the approximate date of the conversation. We will verify ownership and delete the data we control, subject to legal retention requirements.

You may also delete messages directly within Instagram; however, server-side copies required for business records may persist until processed through the request workflow above.
