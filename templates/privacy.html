Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

This Privacy Policy explains how Hİ-MAN ("we", "us") collects and uses information when you interact with our internal business tools and the Instagram Business integration associated with the account @himanistanbul. The application is used solely to manage our own business messages and content; it is not offered to the general public.

Information We Process

  • Instagram Direct Messages sent to or from @himanistanbul (message text and metadata such as sender ID and timestamps).
  • Operational logs required to ensure delivery and troubleshooting.

How We Use Information

  • To receive, store and manage messages for customer support and order-related communications.
  • To improve our internal workflows and ensure service reliability and security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be processed by our infrastructure providers (hosting, storage) strictly to operate the service.

Data Retention

Message data is retained as long as necessary for business operations and record-keeping, then deleted or anonymized.

Security

Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel. Transport uses HTTPS; webhook events are verified with HMAC signatures. Stored data resides in protected databases with access controls.

Your Choices

Instagram users may delete messages from their side or contact us to request deletion of messages we control. See the Data Deletion section for instructions.
